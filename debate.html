Debate

NOTE:
1. Each team shall comprise 2 members.
2. 3 minutes will be given to each speaker to speak for or against the topic.
3. 1 minute rebuttal time will be given to the other participants, to question the speaker.
4. 1 minute rebuttal time will be reserved for the judge for asking questions.
5. The marking scheme will be based on how well the speaker is able to prove her/his point and how efficiently they can tackle the rebuttal questions.
6. Conclusions made by the governing body will be absolute & unchangeable.
